# Privacy Policy — Momentum Reminders

**Effective date:** July 13, 2026

Momentum Reminders ("the Program," "we," "us," or "our") is a low-volume personal productivity SMS program operated by a sole proprietor. This Privacy Policy explains what information the Program collects in connection with its text-messaging service and how that information is used.

## Information We Collect

- **Mobile phone number** — the number a subscriber enrolls to receive reminder messages.
- **Opt-in / consent status** — a record of whether consent to receive messages was given, and whether it has since been withdrawn.
- **Message delivery logs** — records of messages sent and their delivery status (for example: sent, delivered, or failed), used for reliability and support.

## Who Receives Messages

Messages are sent only to phone numbers that have completed the Program's opt-in process — either through the sign-up form on the Program's opt-in page (https://rey3287.github.io/momentum-sms-policy/) or by texting the keyword **START** to +1 (561) 408-4073.

## How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to send the reminder and notification messages you signed up for (appointment reminders, task reminders, study-session reminders, and end-of-day check-in summaries) and to operate, maintain, and troubleshoot the Program. It is not used for advertising or promotional purposes.

**We do not sell, rent, or share any information or mobile data — including mobile phone numbers and consent/opt-in information — with third parties or affiliates for marketing or any other purpose.**

## Messaging Disclosures

Message frequency varies, typically up to ~10 messages per day. Message and data rates may apply. Reply **STOP** to unsubscribe or **HELP** for help at any time.

## Data Retention

We retain your mobile phone number, consent status, and message delivery logs only for as long as the Program is active or as needed to operate it and to meet legal, accounting, or recordkeeping obligations. When this information is no longer needed, it is deleted or de-identified.

## How to Opt Out

You may opt out at any time by replying **STOP** to any message from the Program. After you opt out, automated reminder messages will stop. You can rejoin at any time by texting **START** to +1 (561) 408-4073. For assistance, reply **HELP** to any message or contact us at the address below.
