# Momentum Reminders — SMS Terms & Conditions

_Last updated: June 2026_

**1. Program description.** Momentum Reminders is a personal SMS reminder service. The account owner — a sole proprietor — receives automated task, study, calendar, and appointment reminders sent to their own mobile number. The account owner is the only recipient; no other person is enrolled or messaged.

**2. Message frequency.** Variable, typically up to ~10 messages per day.

**3. Cost.** Message and data rates may apply.

**4. Opt-out.** Reply **STOP** at any time to unsubscribe. Reply **START** to resubscribe.

**5. Help.** Reply **HELP**, or contact romero3287@gmail.com.

**6. Carrier liability.** Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

**7. Privacy.** We do not sell, rent, or share mobile numbers or any mobile information with third parties or affiliates for marketing or any other purpose. See the full [Privacy Policy](https://github.com/Rey3287/momentum-sms-policy/blob/main/PRIVACY.md).
